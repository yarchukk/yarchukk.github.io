Progression

When and how to make things harder

The rule: Advance when you hit the top of the rep range at RPE ≤ 7, two sessions in a row. Don't rush — tendons adapt slower than muscles.

Push progressions

Push-up negatives→ Full push-ups→ Decline push-ups→ Archer push-ups
Dip negatives (wk 1–3)→ Full dips × 5→ Weighted dips
Pike push-ups→ Elevated pike→ Wall handstand

Pull progressions

Pull-ups (~8 now)→ L-sit pull-ups→ Archer pull-ups→ Weighted pull-ups
Tuck front lever→ Advanced tuck→ One-leg FL

Deload — every 6–8 weeks

50% cut in sets and reps for one full week

Tendons genuinely need this planned rest. Don't skip deload weeks — you'll come back stronger every time.
